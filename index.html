Considerations when traveling to Alaska

  1. Wild life is very dangerous and the biggest threat to travelers. Bear spray and bear bells are recomended for all hikers.
  2. Guided tours in Alaska are expensive so make sure you budget for the trip so you have enough money to do fun adventures.
  3. When fishing and hunting make sure you follow all laws. The state does not play around with poaching and Alaska has the most strict poaching laws in the US.
